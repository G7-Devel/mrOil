Before Scheduling

  • Decide on an address within Spokane city limits where You would like your Oil Changed
  • If you would like service on a property not owned by you, please make sure you have have authorization do service your vehicle.

Schedule Appointment

  • Use Link on top of this website to select service address, service options, and appointment date/time convenient for you.
  • Prepay for service selected.

Appointment Day

  • Make sure vehicle is not used for 1/2 hr prior to scheduled appointment time. We don't want our technitians burned from hot engine
  • Make sure vehicle has minumum of 3 feet clearance on all four sides
  • You will receive text message with ETA when our technician is on his way
  • Provide vehicle key to technician
  • When service is complete, technitian will return key and provide electronic receipt
